Why Use Jitsi?

Jitsi is an online conferencing and communication platform. It works just like any other conferencing service- Zoom, Google Meets, etc. However, because there is more data and privacy protection with intricate encryption, Jitsi is the perfect tool for the Animal Liberation Front.

The ALF is known to organize protests, stage “coups” to free animals, and more. Because their methods can be considered unconventional and even violent by governments and corporations, there is always a threat to their privacy. Because Jitsu offers end-to-end encyrption, members of the ALF will be kept anonymous.

Additionally, the press website for the ALF is all contributed to anonymously. By conversing through Jitsi, members can coordinate their thoughts and express them on the website without risking their conversation being leaked or monitored. Since the government sees them as a local terrorist group, data privacy and anonymity is a major concern for the ALF. Jitsi is a perfect way to address this concern.
