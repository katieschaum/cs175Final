ALPO

The Animal Liberation Press Office is an organization dedicated to promoting the ideology of animal liberation and providing a platform for animal rights activists to share information and resources. They act as a media liaison, disseminating news releases, statements, and communications related to action taken on behalf of animals. The organization aims to raise awareness about animal exploitation and inspire activism through the coverage of animal rights issues in the media.
